Attendance IP

Set your library's Public Internet IP. Students must be on your Wi-Fi to check in.

Important: If your library loses power or you reboot your router, your ISP might assign your router a new Public IP address.

If that happens, students will suddenly get blocked. The Fix is easy: You just connect to your library's Wi-Fi, open your Admin Dashboard, and click the "Detect IP" button. It will instantly update the database with the new IP, and students can check in again. (Or, you can ask your ISP for a "Static IP", which never changes, and you'll never have to touch it again).
