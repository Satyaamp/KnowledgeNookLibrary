Code of Conduct

Applicable to all members, visitors, renewals, re-activations, and special or unavoidable activities

1. Purpose

This Code of Conduct is established to maintain a safe, disciplined, respectful, and study-oriented environment within Swadhyaya Sansthan and associated premises. Compliance with this Code is mandatory for all members.

2. General Discipline

  • Members must maintain silence and decorum at all times.
  • Any form of disturbance, shouting, or disruptive behavior is strictly prohibited.
  • Members must follow instructions given by library staff or management.

3. Behavior & Decency

  • Members must behave respectfully towards fellow users, staff, and management.
  • Harassment, intimidation, stalking, offensive conduct, or inappropriate physical behavior is strictly prohibited.
  • Any behavior that creates discomfort or fear among other users will be treated as serious misconduct.

4. Use of Premises

  • Members must restrict their activities strictly to study-related purposes.
  • Misuse of library space, common areas, or building premises is prohibited.
  • Conduct outside the library but within the building that affects library discipline will be considered a violation.

5. Electronic Devices & Internet

  • Mobile phones, tablets, and laptops may be used only for study purposes.
  • Internet access is strictly for educational streaming.
  • Downloading, gaming, or high-data consumption activities are prohibited.

6. Dress & Personal Conduct

  • Members must dress modestly and appropriately for a study environment.
  • Any attire or behavior deemed inappropriate for an academic setting may invite disciplinary action.

7. Safety, Property & CCTV

  • Members must handle library property with care.
  • Damage to property may result in penalties or termination.
  • CCTV surveillance is in operation for safety and discipline and may be used internally for verification.

8. Prohibited Activities

The following are strictly prohibited:

  • Harassment or inappropriate interaction
  • Fighting, threats, or abusive language
  • Substance use or possession (alcohol, drugs, etc.)
  • Any illegal or unethical activity

9. Disciplinary Action

Any violation of this Code may result in: Verbal or written warning | Suspension of membership | Immediate termination without refund | Permanent denial of future enrollment. Repeated or serious violations will attract zero-tolerance action.

10. Acceptance

  • This Code of Conduct is binding on all members.
  • Ignorance of rules shall not be accepted as an excuse.
  • Continued use of the library implies acceptance of this Code.
  • Management reserves the right to interpret, enforce, and amend this Code of Conduct as required.
